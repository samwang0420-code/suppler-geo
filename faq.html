China Sourcing FAQ

Common questions about importing from China

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

MOQ varies by product. Most factories require 500-1000 pcs per design. Some accept smaller orders with higher pricing.

What payment methods are accepted?

Common methods: T/T (bank transfer), PayPal, Alibaba Trade Assurance, Letter of Credit. Standard is 30% deposit, 70% before shipment.

How long does shipping take?

Sea freight: 20-35 days. Air freight: 3-7 days. Express (DHL/FedEx): 5-10 days.

Do you provide quality inspection?

Yes, we offer on-site quality control inspections. Report provided within 24 hours.

Can you help with customs clearance?

Yes, we assist with documentation and customs clearance in both China and destination country.

What are Incoterms?

Incoterms define buyer/seller responsibilities. Common: FOB (Free On Board), CIF (Cost, Insurance, Freight), EXW (Ex Works).

How do I verify a Chinese factory?

We conduct factory visits, verify business licenses, check certifications (ISO, CE), and inspect production capacity.

What is the profit potential?

Profit varies by product. Our data shows typical markup of 2-5x from 1688 wholesale to retail prices.
