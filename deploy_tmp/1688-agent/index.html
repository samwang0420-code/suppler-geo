Why You Can't Use 1688 Directly

1688.com is Alibaba's Chinese sibling — even bigger catalog, even lower prices. But for international buyers, it's nearly impossible to use.

🗣️

Interface in Chinese Only

No English. No Google Translate works well enough. Product pages, seller profiles, negotiations — all in Chinese.

💳

Requires Chinese Payment

1688 only accepts Alipay CN, Chinese bank cards, or enterprise accounts. Foreign cards are rejected at checkout.

🏭

Seller Verification Hard

Many sellers don't ship internationally. Factory vs. trader distinction is unclear. No transparent review system for foreign buyers.

📦

Complex Domestic Shipping

Even if you buy, getting goods from Chinese warehouses to international addresses requires a domestic freight forwarder — another layer of complexity.

⚖️

Dispute Resolution Risk

If product is wrong or missing, Chinese platform dispute process requires Chinese documents and communication. Near impossible without local help.

📸

Product Quality Verification

You can't check what's actually in the package before it leaves China. One fake product can ruin your entire order.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I really buy from 1688 even if I don't speak Chinese?

Yes. We handle the entire process. You send us the product link or description, we translate, verify, place the order, and ship to you. You never need to interact with 1688 directly or speak a word of Chinese.

What's the minimum order I can place?

理论上 1688 sellers have MOQs (Minimum Order Quantities), but many products on 1688 allow orders as low as 1-5 pieces for some items. For very small orders (under $200 total), our service fee is 5%. The sweet spot for cost efficiency is orders above $500 where the service fee becomes very manageable per unit.

How long does shipping take?

From China to most countries: DHL (3-5 days), UPS (5-7 days), sea freight (25-45 days). We always give you the actual shipping time and cost estimate before you commit. We work with DHL, FedEx, UPS, and sea freight forwarders.

What if the product is wrong or damaged?

Our pre-shipment photo and video verification means we catch problems before they reach you. If an issue is found, we negotiate with the seller on your behalf and handle the dispute. We keep detailed records of all orders, communications, and QC photos as evidence.

Can you help me find products I'm looking for on 1688?

Absolutely. If you have an image, a product name in English, or even a rough description, we can search 1688 for you, compare sellers, verify factory status, and present you with options. This is part of our sourcing service.

Is 1688 cheaper than Alibaba?

Generally yes — 1688 prices are 10-30% lower than Alibaba for the same products because 1688 targets domestic Chinese buyers. Alibaba adds a premium for international buyer support, English interface, and buyer protection. With our 3-5% service fee, you still typically pay less buying from 1688 through us than directly on Alibaba.

How do I pay you?

We accept PayPal, bank wire transfer (USD/EUR/GBP), and major credit cards. You pay us in your currency — we handle the CNY conversion internally. No foreign exchange headaches for you.

Do you offer dropshipping?

Yes. We can connect you with suppliers who offer dropshipping on 1688, handle the order fulfillment, and ship directly to your customers. This is popular for e-commerce sellers on Shopify, eBay, and Amazon. Contact us to discuss your specific needs.
