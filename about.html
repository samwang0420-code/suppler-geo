About Us

Your trusted partner for China sourcing and project management

📍 Service Areas

Serving clients in California (Los Angeles, San Francisco, San Diego), Texas, and Florida

Your Project Manager, Your Advocate

SupplyLink was founded to solve a simple problem: US construction companies need access to affordable custom hardware and materials from China, but dealing with factories directly is difficult, risky, and time-consuming.

We're here to be your eyes and ears in China. As your dedicated project manager, we handle the complexities of sourcing, quality control, and logistics — so you can focus on what you do best: building.

We understand the US construction industry. We speak your language, understand your specs, and know what it takes to deliver parts that meet American building codes and expectations.

Why US Contractors Choose Us

🎯 We Speak Your Language

No more confusing factory communications. We translate your requirements into clear manufacturing specs. You get one point of contact who understands construction.

🛡️ Quality Guaranteed

Every order is inspected before shipping. We verify materials, dimensions, and finish. You receive photo/video reports and certificates of compliance.

⏱️ Fast Turnaround

Express samples in 7-14 days. Production in 2-4 weeks. Air shipping available for urgent needs. We work with your timeline.

💰 No MOQ Requirements

Whether you need 50 pieces or 50,000, we find the right factory. We handle small batches with the same care as large orders.

📦 Complete Logistics

From factory to your door. We handle freight, customs, and delivery. Track your order every step of the way.

🤝 Long-Term Partnership

We're not a one-time sourcing agent. We aim to be your ongoing partner, understanding your needs and preferences over time.
